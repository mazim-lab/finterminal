    Switch to Monthly Online Statements (eStatements)



    




    
Login to EasyWeb Online banking
If you do not have an EasyWeb Online banking login, please Register now.
Select the Settings & Services menu (👤) in the top right of the screen.
Select Statements & Documents. 
Go to the Manage tab, and then click on Edit your delivery preferences.
To switch all accounts to online delivery, select the Switch ALL to online checkbox.
Select the Save Changes button to save your delivery preferences.
Accept the Consent to Electronic Delivery of Documents agreement.
