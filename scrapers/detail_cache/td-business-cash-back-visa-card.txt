Security
Chip & PIN technology:
TD Visa* Cards with Chip and PIN technology provide an added level of security through the use of a Personal Identification Number (PIN). A PIN makes it much more difficult for unauthorized users to copy or access the information on your TD Credit Card.
Purchase Security and Extended Warranty Protection5:
You automatically have access to Purchase Security and Extended Warranty Protection, covering most eligible new items you’ve purchased with your Card, should they be stolen or damaged within 90 days of purchase. If the item comes with a manufacturer’s warranty valid in Canada, you may be entitled to double the warranty period for up to 12 additional months.
Visa Secure* : Visa Secure provides you with increased security and convenience when you shop online through In-app Authentication. Learn more.
Instant alerts. Added convenience, with TD Fraud Alerts:
You can now automatically receive TD Fraud Alerts to your mobile phone any time we suspect unusual activity on your TD Business Cash Back Visa* Card. Simply ensure that we have your current mobile phone number listed in your TD Customer Profile. Learn more.
